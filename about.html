About Mili

Mili is a Japanese indie music group formed in August 2012. They have contributed their music to various media like video games, anime, and commercials.

Video Games:

  • Library of Ruina
  • Ender Lilies: Quietus of the Knights
  • Limbus Company
  • Arknights
  • Promise of Wizard
  • Snowbreak: Containment Zone

Anime:

  • Goblin Slayer
  • Ghost in the Shell: SAC_2045
  • Gleipnir
